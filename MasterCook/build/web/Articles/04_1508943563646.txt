<p class="MsoNormal">500g pack dried whole fig, hard stalk and centre of base
removed<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal">85g dried apricot, chopped into small pieces<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal">50g dried cranberries<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal">1 tbsp brandy<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal">1 tbsp clear honey<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal">1 tsp ground cloves<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal">100g sesame seed, toasted<o:p></o:p></p><h2 class="responsive-tabs__pane-title" style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; font-family: SerifaBT-Light, Georgia, "Times New Roman", Times, serif; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); text-rendering: optimizeLegibility; font-size: 25px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 10px; background-image: url("/sites/all/themes/bbcw_goodfood/images/ui/border-dash.3.gif?vcea4046353d3aff5c44a4d7f021d9f80") !important; background-position: left bottom !important; background-size: initial !important; background-repeat: repeat-x !important; background-attachment: initial !important; background-origin: initial !important; background-clip: initial !important;">Method</h2></div><div><ol class="method__list" style="margin-right: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; padding: 0px; list-style: none; font-family: "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, Arial; font-size: 15px;"><li class="method__item" itemprop="recipeInstructions" style="line-height: 1.6; font-size: 16px; border-bottom: 1px solid rgb(240, 242, 235); counter-increment: method 1; margin-bottom: 20px; padding-bottom: 20px;"><p style="line-height: 1.5; margin-bottom: 0px; padding-bottom: 0px; padding-top: 2px;">1. Whizz the almonds in a food processor until most are finely chopped, then tip into a large bowl. Roughly chop the figs, then whizz to a smooth sticky paste. Scrape onto the almonds then, using your hands, mix together well with the dried fruit, brandy, honey and cloves.</p></li><li class="method__item" itemprop="recipeInstructions" style="line-height: 1.6; font-size: 16px; border-bottom: 1px solid rgb(240, 242, 235); counter-increment: method 1; padding-bottom: 20px; margin-bottom: 0px !important;"><p style="line-height: 1.5; margin-bottom: 0px; padding-bottom: 0px; padding-top: 2px;">2. Divide the mixture into 6 and roll into balls. Tip the sesame seeds onto a tray, then roll the balls in them until covered. Cover the tray loosely with a clean tea towel, then leave the fig balls to dry for a week before packaging. Will keep in a cool place for 2 months.</p></li></ol></div>
