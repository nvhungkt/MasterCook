<p class="MsoNormal">1/8 teaspoon caraway seeds <o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal">1 pound fresh bratwurst sausages <o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal">1 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle lager beer <o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal">1 tablespoon light brown sugar <o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal">2 teaspoons dry mustard powder <o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal">1 teaspoon onion powder <o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal">1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper <o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal">1/8 teaspoon dried dill weed <o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal">1 pound sauerkraut (preferably barrel-aged), drained<o:p></o:p></p><h2 class="responsive-tabs__pane-title" style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; font-family: SerifaBT-Light, Georgia, "Times New Roman", Times, serif; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); text-rendering: optimizeLegibility; font-size: 25px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 10px; background-image: url("/sites/all/themes/bbcw_goodfood/images/ui/border-dash.3.gif?vcea4046353d3aff5c44a4d7f021d9f80") !important; background-position: left bottom !important; background-size: initial !important; background-repeat: repeat-x !important; background-attachment: initial !important; background-origin: initial !important; background-clip: initial !important;">Method</h2></div><div><ol class="method__list" style="margin-right: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; padding: 0px; list-style: none; font-family: "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, Arial; font-size: 15px;"><li class="method__item" itemprop="recipeInstructions" style="line-height: 1.6; font-size: 16px; border-bottom: 1px solid rgb(240, 242, 235); counter-increment: method 1; margin-bottom: 20px; padding-bottom: 20px;"><p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 36pt; line-height: 15pt; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;"><span style="font-family: Helvetica, sans-serif; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">1. Crush celery seeds and
caraway seeds in a mortar and pestle until ground; set aside.<o:p></o:p></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 36pt; line-height: 15pt; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;"><span style="font-family: Helvetica, sans-serif; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">2. Place bratwursts into a
large nonstick skillet over medium heat. Mix beer, brown sugar, dry mustard,
onion powder, black pepper, dill weed, and crushed celery and caraway seeds in
a bowl, stirring to dissolve brown sugar; pour over the brats. Bring to a boil
and reduce heat to low; cover and simmer for 10 minutes.<o:p></o:p></span></p><p class="MsoNormal">



</p><p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 36pt; line-height: 15pt; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;"><span style="font-family: Helvetica, sans-serif; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">3. Uncover and raise heat
to medium; boil the sauce until it reduces to a thick, syrupy liquid, about 20
minutes. Tilt skillet to roll brats in the sticky sauce until thoroughly
coated. Transfer sausage to a platter and keep warm. Cook and stir sauerkraut
in the same skillet to mix in any leftover sauce, 5 to 8 minutes. Transfer
sauerkraut to a serving platter and top with cooked bratwurst.<o:p></o:p></span></p></li></ol></div>
