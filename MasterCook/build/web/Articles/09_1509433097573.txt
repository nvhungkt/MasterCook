<p class="MsoNormal">2-3 spring onion, sliced<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal">50g fresh or frozen petits pois<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal">85g Chilli, coriander & lime butter<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal">2 x 150g salmon fillets<o:p></o:p></p><h2 class="responsive-tabs__pane-title" style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; font-family: SerifaBT-Light, Georgia, "Times New Roman", Times, serif; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); text-rendering: optimizeLegibility; font-size: 25px; padding-top: 20px; padding-bottom: 10px; background-image: url("/sites/all/themes/bbcw_goodfood/images/ui/border-dash.3.gif?vcea4046353d3aff5c44a4d7f021d9f80") !important; background-position: left bottom !important; background-size: initial !important; background-repeat: repeat-x !important; background-attachment: initial !important; background-origin: initial !important; background-clip: initial !important;">Method</h2></div><div><ol class="method__list" style="margin-right: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; padding: 0px; list-style: none; font-family: "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, Arial; font-size: 15px;"><li class="method__item" itemprop="recipeInstructions" style="line-height: 1.6; font-size: 16px; border-bottom: 1px solid rgb(240, 242, 235); counter-increment: method 1; margin-bottom: 20px; padding-bottom: 20px;"><p style="line-height: 1.5; margin-bottom: 0px; padding-bottom: 0px; padding-top: 2px;">1. Heat oven to 200C/fan 180C/gas 6. Put the noodles in a bowl and pour over enough boiling water to just cover them. Leave for 2 mins, until they are bendable, but not too soft, then drain well. Return to the bowl with the spring onions, petit pois, salt and pepper and a third of the butter. Mix well until the butter has melted.</p></li><li class="method__item" itemprop="recipeInstructions" style="line-height: 1.6; font-size: 16px; border-bottom: 1px solid rgb(240, 242, 235); counter-increment: method 1; padding-bottom: 20px; margin-bottom: 0px !important;"><p style="line-height: 1.5; margin-bottom: 0px; padding-bottom: 0px; padding-top: 2px;">2. Pile the noodles and vegetables onto two large squares of baking parchment, then sit the salmon on top. Slice the remaining butter and arrange over the top of the salmon. Bring the ends of the paper over the fish, fold them together to seal, then tuck the ends of the paper underneath so there are no gaps. Put the parcels on a baking sheet and bake for 15-20 mins. Transfer to dinner plates and serve without delay.</p></li></ol></div>
